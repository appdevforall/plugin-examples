AI Literacy Course

An offline Introduction to AI course from Learn AI Anywhere, packaged for Code On The Go. Twenty-six short lesson videos plus interactive activities, the NeuroPocket app, and teacher resources — all running on-device with no internet required.

How to open it. Open the side menu and choose AI Literacy Course. The course opens full-screen.
First run unpacks once. The first time you open the course it unpacks its videos and activities into the plugin's private storage — this takes a few seconds and shows a progress screen. Every open after that is instant, online or off.

What's inside

  • Introduction — course trailer, prerequisites, and the symbol key.
  • Lessons 1–4 — objectives, teaching videos, a recap activity, a project-time video, and a worksheet, in playback order.
  • Activities — self-check quizzes and worksheets (HTML), plus the NeuroPocket machine-learning app that runs entirely in the browser.
  • Resources — teacher guide, slide deck, and printable worksheets (PDF).

Using the course

Videos play inline; tap the fullscreen control to fill the screen, and rotate the device for a wider view. Activities and PDFs open in place — use the device Back gesture to return to the lesson list. Back also exits a fullscreen video first, then steps back through the course, then closes the course screen.

Offline by design

The entire course is bundled inside the plugin and served locally, so it works the same with the network off. Nothing is uploaded, and no learner data leaves the device — the self-check activities keep their scores on-device only.
